Intro

I've always been fascinated by patterns. They got me into music... from elementary-school concert band, to high-school garage band, to college marching band, to a bluegrass and jazz band. Patterns also got me into languages, from German, to Chinese, to Portuguese and others. I studied patterns in people and cultures during a master degree in social work, 8-year career as a therapist, and living in/visited dozens of foreign countries for a total of over 3 years. I love communicating ideas and solving problems through patterns of music, language, and human behavior!

It turns out, I've also always used patterns in some form of coding on the side... from the card game "21" on my graphing calculator in 11th grade, to blogs and dynamic spreadsheet expressions, to volunteering as 'Webmaster' in a non-profit organization for more than 5 years. Ultimately, I decided to devote myself specifically to developing skills and using patterns in coding. I completed a 6-month intensive full-stack coding bootcamp at App Academy. Feel free to check out some of my work!

Work

eDOT

Electronic Diagnostic and Outcome Testing for therapists and other helping professionals. A full stack solo project using Node.js, React, Redux, Flask, SQL-Alchemy, Chart.js and more. Checkout the github repo.

Toilet Surfing

Where chill people share their toilets with other chill people, inspired by the giving and laid-back culture of couch surfing and by good vibes of surfers in general. A full stack solo project using Node.js, React, Redux, Express, AWS S3, React Google Maps API and more. Checkout the github repo.

Pair Yo' Pet

From play dates to soul mates, your pet will never be lonely again. A full stack group project using React, Redux, Flask, SQL-Alchemy, AWS S3 and more. Checkout the github repo.

Good Trees

For those who love the climb and the view from the top. A full stack group project using Node.js, Pug, Express, PostgreSQL and more. Checkout the github repo.
